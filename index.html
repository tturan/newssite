First Peacock Spotted in 27 Years

Eastapple Island residents reported spotting a peacock in the Northwest Beachforests. This is the first time a peacock has ever been spotted in the area in 27 years. The discovery brings hope to the residents that the island's natural habitat is reconstructing itself after the catastrophic Event-X.
